MARK-B.L.U.

MARK-B.L.U. is a quantum-hybrid security architecture designed for protecting agentic AI systems and autonomous infrastructures against post-classical threat models. It integrates quantum randomness, entropic validation, and QPU-backed identity primitives to provide verifiable trust and secure communication pipelines.


The framework explores entropy-backed authentication, quantum-temporal identity graphs, and secure channels between distributed AI agents, enabling resilience in high-autonomy environments.



Core Themes

  • Quantum-driven entropy extraction
  • Adaptive cryptographic primitives
  • Identity graphs for agentic systems
  • Secure autonomous infrastructure
